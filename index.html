Khoza MD Consulting Was Established in 2023

With over 40 years of combined HR consulting experience, our company is dedicated to providing businesses with reliable, strategic, and results-driven HR solutions. Led by a team of seasoned professionals, including Mr. Carlton, whose expertise in Labour and Industrial Relations stems from his leadership roles at WAMU (NUMSA) and HHA, we are equipped to support businesses of all sizes.

  • 40+ years of industry experience offering comprehensive HR services tailored to your business needs.
  • Expert guidance in Labour and Industrial Relations, with a team that brings real-world solutions to the table.
  • Proven track record with key industry experience at WAMU (NUMSA) and HHA (Hendry Holland Associates) supporting businesses since 1996.

Our company combines decades of expertise to deliver strategic HR consulting that fosters growth, improves organizational performance, and ensures a positive workplace culture for your business.
